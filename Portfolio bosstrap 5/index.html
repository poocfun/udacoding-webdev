About Me

A programmer is a person who writes code or computer programs. They use programming languages such as Python, Java, C++, and others to build software applications, websites, and other systems. Programmers must have a strong understanding of computer science principles and be able to translate a client or business's needs into a functional and efficient program!

They also test and debug their code, and work with other team members to resolve technical problems. Programmers play a crucial role in the development and implementation of technology in today's society.
